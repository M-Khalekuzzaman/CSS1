University of Rajshahi

The University of Rajshahi, also known as Rajshahi University or RU Bengali: রাজশাহী বিশ্ববিদ্যালয়. The university's 60 departments are organised into ten faculties.

Rajshahi University is in a 305-hectare campus in Motihar, 3 kilometres from the Rajshahi city centre. It is the second largest, in terms of academic activities and second oldest university in Bangladesh. Currently is the best research university in Bangladesh. Researchers of this university recently put significant effort and played a key role in bringing back Banladeshi Muslin fiber.
